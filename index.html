About CR Mechanics Ltd.
We are your trusted, heavy-duty mechanic shop.

CR Mechanics Ltd. is your trusted, fully professional, heavy-duty mechanics shop serving Southern Alberta. Located just outside Picture Butte, and only 15 minutes from Lethbridge, we are centrally located in the heart of the agricultural community. Our experienced, Red Seal certified team works on the leading edge of developments in automotive technology, blending decades of hands-on experience with the latest software applications. We are trusted, we are professional, and we stand by our work.

Cam Reiter is a certified Red Seal mechanic with over 21 years of experience . Educated at the Southern Alberta Institute of Technology and Lethbridge College, Cam earned his place in the industry by working at Case IH for three years, followed by another three years with John Deere. Fifteen years ago he founded CR Mechanics Ltd. and the business has grown and thrived since.


Cam and his team also care deeply about giving back to the community in which they live and work, regularly investing in the local schools to give young people new opportunities to learn, and into local culture and events by supporting the local tractor club and donating regularly to charitable organizations.


Cam and his Red Seal team are experienced with every aspect of the trade, trusted by their customers, and they stand by their work.
